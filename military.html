RQ-2A Pionner (USA)

In the 1980s, U.S.A military operations in Libya, for example, identified a need for an inexpensive, unmanned, reconnaissance, and able to deal damage. As a result, in July 1985, the Navy make the acquisition of UAV systems for fleet operations using non-developmental technology. Two Pioneer systems were procured in December 1985 for testing during 1986. Initial system delivery was made in July 1986 and subsequently deployed on the battleship in December 1986.
